Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my counter?

Tap the reset button (circular arrow icon) on the main screen.

How do I change the prayer time location?

The app uses your device location automatically. Make sure location permission is granted in iOS Settings.

Subscription

How do I unlock all premium features?

Open the side menu and tap "Unlimited" to view subscription options.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Open Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone, find Hachi, and tap Cancel.

Privacy

Is my data shared?

No personal data is sent to any server. See our Privacy Policy for details.
