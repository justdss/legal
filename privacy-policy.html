Privacy Policy

App: Hachi
Last updated: April 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Hachi ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

Hachi does not collect, store, or share any personal data on external servers, with the exceptions described below.

The following device features may be used:

  • Location (GPS) — used solely to determine the Qibla direction. Location data is never stored or transmitted.
  • Notifications — used to send prayer time reminders. Notification settings are stored locally on your device only.

2. Data Storage

All app data (settings, counters, preferences, statistics) is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. No data is sent to any server unless explicitly stated below.

3. Contact Us Feature

When you use the "Contact Us" feature in the app, the message you voluntarily submit is sent to our Firebase Firestore server for the sole purpose of receiving your feedback. We do not collect any personal information (such as your name, email address, or device ID) beyond the text you choose to send.

4. Third-Party Services

Hachi uses the following third-party services:

  • Aladhan API — to calculate prayer times based on your location. Only your coordinates are sent to this service to retrieve prayer times. No personal data is shared.
  • Google AdMob — to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Firestore — used to receive feedback messages submitted via the "Contact Us" feature. Governed by Google's Firebase Privacy Policy.

5. Children's Privacy

Hachi is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support.hachi@gmail.com
